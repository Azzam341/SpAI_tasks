Muhammad Tariq Abbasi, J — This single judgment is intended to

decide the above captioned Murder reference and the Criminal
Appeal as the both are outcome of single judgment dated 28.7.2010,

passed by the learned Additional Sessions Judge, Rawalpindi.

2. Through the above mentioned judgment, in a criminal case
registered through FIR No. 361 dated 16.5.2008 under Sections
302/34 of PPC, at Police Station Civil Lines, Rawalpindi, towards
commission of Qatl-i-Amd of Ibrar Hussain, the appellant has been
convicted and sentenced to death, with compensation of
Rs.2,00,000/- payable to the legal heirs of the deceased, failing

which to undergo 06 months S.I.
